The Review was started in 1933 by a group of young British and American Economists. It is
published by The Review of Economic Studies Ltd., whose object is to encourage research in
theoretical and applied economics, especially by young economists, and to publish the results in the
Review of Economic Studies.
The Review also administers a travel fund to enable young economists or econometricians
travelling either to or from the U.K. to present papers at conferences and workshops. Applications
should be made to the Business Manager, Dr. Alan Hamlin, Department of Economics, University
of Southampton, Southampton S017 1BJ.
