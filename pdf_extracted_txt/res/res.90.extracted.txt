THE REVIEW OF ECONOMIC STUDIES
The review was started in 1933 by a group of young British and American Economists. It is published by The Review of Economic
Studies Ltd, whose object is to encourage research in theoretical and applied economics, especially by young economists, and to
publish the results in The Review of Economic Studies.
